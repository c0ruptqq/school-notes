### Ideal gas formula

PV = nRT

P - Pressure in Pa

V - Volume in $m^3$

n - Moles

R - Ideal gas constant (8.31 $Jk^{-1}mol^{-1}$)

T - Temperature in kelvin

One atm (atmosphere) = 101325 Pa
